Gloy Weather

Get weather updates quickly and beautifully. Just ask for your city or town, and Gloy delivers instant forecasts in an elegant, easy-to-read format.

Today we are introducing Gloy Weather — a fast, beautiful way to check the weather without leaving your conversation. No apps to install, no websites to navigate. Just ask Gloy, and get your forecast instantly.

How It Works

Using Gloy Weather is effortless. Simply tell Gloy your city or town, and the weather appears in a clean, visually appealing window. Current conditions, temperature, and key details are displayed at a glance.

Instant Results

Get weather information immediately. No waiting, no loading screens — just ask and receive.

Any Location

Works with cities, towns, and villages worldwide. Just name your place and Gloy finds it.

Today & Tomorrow

Need tomorrow's forecast? Just ask. Gloy provides both current and next-day predictions.

Beautiful Design

Weather data presented in an elegant, easy-to-read visual format that fits Gloy's aesthetic.

Tomorrow's Weather Too

Planning ahead? Gloy Weather can provide forecasts for tomorrow as well. Just specify how many days you need — whether it's just today, tomorrow, or a short-range outlook.

Example queries: "What's the weather in Paris?" / "Weather in my village" / "Forecast for tomorrow in Tokyo" / "Weather for next 2 days in London"

Ask for weather in any city — Gloy displays current conditions and today's forecast in a beautiful, easy-to-read format

Powered by Forecast API

Gloy Weather currently operates through Forecast API, providing reliable and accurate weather data. This integration ensures you receive up-to-date information from trusted meteorological sources.

Technical note: Forecast API integration is currently active on our side. This means weather functionality is available immediately without requiring any API keys or setup from users. We are working to bring this capability fully in-house soon.

Try It Now

Gloy Weather is available right now. Open your conversation with Gloy and ask about the weather in your area. Experience how simple and elegant weather updates can be.
